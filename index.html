The Dark Arts of SEO

What is search?

Why is search important?

Without search, discoverability on the world wide web would be limited to the places you know and the places you've been, along with any hyperlinks in those places.


Search engines make the web a much more powerful tool.

Search Engines Work in Three Ways:

  1. Crawlers
  2. Indexing
  3. Retrieval

Crawling

Page title, keywords, images, and links, at minimum.

Any newly discovered links are flagged to be crawled next.

Only TOR-hosted sites are unindexed by Google.

Indexing

All of the data collected by the spiders is indexed into the database which is distributed across the globe in datacenters with thousands of petabytes of drives.

Retrieval

  • Relevance - Your query is checked against billions of results to determine relevancy
  • Popularity - The value of your information: how many shares does your site have? How many links from other sites?

Retrieval

  • Relevance - Semantic Markup
  • Popularity - Great Content
